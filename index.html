Frequently Asked Questions

What is RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning)?

RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) is a process that allows individuals to gain a nationally recognized qualification based on their existing skills, knowledge, and experience, regardless of how or where they were acquired.

How long does the RPL process take?

The duration of the RPL process can vary depending on the complexity of your experience and the responsiveness in providing documentation. Generally, it can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months.

Do you issue the qualifications directly?

No, Career Bridge Solutions is not an RTO (Registered Training Organisation). We are an independent support system that guides you through the RPL process and connects you with legitimate RTOs who can assess your skills and issue qualifications.
